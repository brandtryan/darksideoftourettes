The Dark Side of Tourettes

Most are familar with Tourette's Syndrome, specifically with the outlandish tics such as coprolalia and copropraxia. However, few are aware of the darker manifistations of the sydnrome. Indeed, if one researches this neurological disease, they will come up short finding anything related to tics of the darker nature. As these "darker" tics, which seem to be limited to the motor variety, are rarely, if ever documented, I've come up with a few terms to categorize them:

  • Ruinapraxia: Ruinous
  • Noxapraxia: Harmful
  • Ultimpraxia: Fatal
